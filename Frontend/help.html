📘 Campus Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use the OLITS portal effectively.

📝 Reporting Items

1. Category & Location: If your item category or location isn't listed, select "Other" to provide specific details.
2. Contact Information: Ensure your phone number is entered correctly with the proper country code for quick reachability.
3. Security PIN: Always set a unique 4-digit PIN. This is required to manage your report and verify your identity during the handover process.

🔍 Search & Filter

Use the filters on the Dashboard to narrow down results by category, location, or date range. Items reported within the last 24 hours will be highlighted with a NEW badge.

🤝 The Handover Process

If a match is found between a lost and found report, follow these steps to securely return the item:

1. Handover: Finders MUST take the item to the Admin Office immediately for safe-keeping.
2. Status Tracking: Once the Admin receives the item, its status will be updated to "Received by Admin".
3. Verification: Owners must visit the office and provide their Security PIN to claim their item.

🚦 Understanding Statuses

  • 🟡 Pending: The system is searching for a matching report.
  • 🟡 Matched: A potential match has been identified!
  • 🔵 Received by Admin: The item is securely held at the Admin Office.
  • 🟢 Verified & Claimed: The item has been successfully returned to the owner. 🎉
  • 🔴 Rejected / Spam: The report was found to be invalid or fraudulent.

🛡️ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is the Security PIN important?
A: It serves as your proof of ownership. The system and the Admin office use it to ensure the item is returned to the correct person.

Q: I found an item, what are my responsibilities?
A: Report the item on the portal immediately and hand it over to the Admin office to ensure its safety and proper documentation.

Q: What happens in case of false reporting?
A: Any attempt to file fake reports or claim items fraudulently is a serious violation and will lead to disciplinary action as per campus policy.
